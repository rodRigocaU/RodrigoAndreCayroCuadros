I am a computer scientist and Master’s student at the University of São Paulo (USP), specializing in computer graphics, GPU programming, and 3D procedural generation. I develop interactive and educational systems through game development, working with technologies like C/C++, Python, C#, Unity, Unreal Engine, and modern graphics APIs. My background includes research, teaching, and open-source projects, with experience in real-time rendering, algorithms, data structures, and computational optimization. I have presented work at international conferences such as CSCI and WiP, and contributed as technical staff at events like CppCon, ADC, and CVPR.
