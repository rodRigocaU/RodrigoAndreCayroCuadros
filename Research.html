Terrain Generation

Terrain Generation using Neural Transfer Style and Fractal Brownian Motion Noise with Constraints

Hybrid terrain generation method combining neural style transfer, fractal noise, and user-defined constraints to produce realistic and controllable terrains for games and simulations.

Accepted and presented as a research paper and poster at WiP 2025 Conference: “Terrain Generation using Neural Transfer Style and Fractal Brownian Motion Noise with Constraints”.

Terrain Simplification

Simplified 3D Terrain Meshes using Poisson Disk Sampling and Perlin Noise on GPU

GPU-based terrain generation method combining Poisson Disk Sampling and Perlin Noise to produce simplified, scalable 3D terrains with significantly reduced mesh complexity.

Accepted paper at the CSCI 2024 Conference: “Simplified 3D Terrain Meshes using Poisson Disk Sampling and Perlin Noise on GPU”.
